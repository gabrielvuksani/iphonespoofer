iPhone Location Spoofer

Change your iPhone's GPS location in one click.

Step 1 of 3

Enable Developer Mode

This is required for your iPhone to accept simulated locations. It's a built-in Apple feature — safe to enable.

1Open Settings on your iPhone
2Go to Privacy & Security
3Scroll down and tap Developer Mode
4Toggle it ON and restart your iPhone when prompted
Don't see Developer Mode? Make sure your iPhone is running iOS 16 or later.
Step 2 of 3

Connect & Trust

Plug your iPhone into your computer with a USB cable.

1Connect your iPhone via USB cable (Lightning or USB-C)
2Unlock your iPhone
3Tap "Trust" when the "Trust This Computer?" dialog appears
4Enter your iPhone passcode to confirm
No dialog? Unplug and replug the cable, or go to Settings → General → Transfer or Reset → Reset Location & Privacy.
Step 3 of 3

You're All Set

Here's how to use the app:

Click the map to place a marker, then press Warp
Turn on Teleport mode to set GPS with a single click
Search any address or place name in the top bar
Shift+click multiple points to simulate movement along a route
iOS 18+: Keep your phone plugged in while spoofing. The location resets when unplugged.
